PediGen

Super Simple and free to use HTML Pedigree Generator. This simple interface allows you to input your animal's details (and meta information depending on species) and will generate an HTML pedigree which you can use for printing.

Background

This whole project started when my daughter was trying to find a tool to generate pedigrees for her bunnies. After some online searches turned up virtually no good results; many of which were trying to charge a premium for very dated solutions. So we decided to roll our own solution and PediGen was developed. Anyone who finds this tool useful should check out her site: http://chloeshollandlops.weebly.com.
